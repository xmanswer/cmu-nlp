Details are still being worked out, but the weekend's highlight looks like the three-hour ``NBC's 75th Anniversary Special,'' airing at 8 on Sunday. ``Saturday Night Live'' honcho Lorne Michaels is producing the show, originating from ``SNL's'' Studio 8H in New York, and while we're not expecting Johnny Carson or David Letterman to show up, just about every other surviving NBC star will, including Bill Cosby, Jerry Seinfeld and Jay Leno.
