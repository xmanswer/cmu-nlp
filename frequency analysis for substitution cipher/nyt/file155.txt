Its nasty proxy fight behind it, its civil trial over allegations of fraud and corruption seemingly complete, Hewlett-Packard Co. finally moves on to the business of doing business. In an industry built on machines, the biggest challenge H-P and Compaq Computer Corp. face is people. Some employees have resigned amid the rancorous infighting over the deal.
