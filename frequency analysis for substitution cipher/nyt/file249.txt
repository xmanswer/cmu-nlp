BLAKE-NOBAIL (Los Angeles) -- Bail was denied Wednesday for Robert Blake, the Emmy Award-winning actor charged with capital murder in the fatal shooting of his wife. Defense attorney Harland Braun had said his client wouldn't be a flight risk and had asked for $1 million bail. Prosecutors detailed more of their case in their motion to deny bail for Blake. HERO-MEDAL (Washington) -- In a Rose Garden ceremony, Dr. Robert West received the Medal of Honor on Wednesday on behalf of Army Capt. Ben Louis Salomon, a fellow WWII vet, University of Southern California grad and dentist, who was killed during the battle for Saipan in 1944. West and his wife, Gloria, received the medal -- the nation's highest military honor -- from President Bush. By Bill Hillburg. (Will move in the ``n'' California and ``a'' national news files.)
