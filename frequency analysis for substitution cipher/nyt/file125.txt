CIR-ONLINE-EDUCATION (Undated) _ At the height of the Internet boom, many colleges started ventures aimed at cashing in on online education _ figuring that it would be ``easy, inexpensive to develop and highly profitable,'' as one expert put it. All three assumptions turned out to be mistaken, and now many of the operations are being rethought or abandoned. By Katie Hafner. 	CIR-ROBOT-DOGS (Los Angeles) _ Aibos, the first mass-produced entertainment robot, have grown in popularity in the three years since they were introduced, with more than 100,000 of the creatures _ which cost from $850 to $1,500 _ sold worldwide. By Eric A. Taub.
